Signs Your Basement Needs Waterproofing

Don't wait until you have standing water. These warning signs indicate you need professional waterproofing:

  • Water stains on basement walls or floor
  • Musty odors or visible mold and mildew
  • Efflorescence (white mineral deposits) on concrete
  • Peeling paint or deteriorating wall surfaces
  • Cracks in foundation walls or floor
  • Damp or humid feeling in the basement
  • Pooling water after rain or snowmelt
  • Sump pump running constantly

Frequently Asked Questions About Basement Waterproofing in Detroit

Why is my Detroit basement wet?

Common causes include hydrostatic pressure from high water tables, poor drainage around the foundation, cracks in walls or floor, failed or missing waterproofing membranes, and clogged or failed sump pumps. Michigan's freeze-thaw cycles and clay soils contribute to basement water problems.

How much does basement waterproofing cost in Detroit?

Costs vary based on the solution needed. Interior waterproofing with French drains typically costs $3,000-$10,000. Exterior waterproofing ranges from $10,000-$25,000 or more depending on foundation size and access. We provide detailed estimates for every project.

Is interior or exterior waterproofing better?

Exterior waterproofing addresses water at its source and is the most comprehensive solution. Interior waterproofing manages water that enters and is less disruptive and costly. The best approach depends on your specific situation‚Äîwe'll evaluate and recommend the right solution.

How long does basement waterproofing take?

Interior French drain installation typically takes 2-4 days depending on basement size. Sump pump installation is usually 1 day. Exterior waterproofing requires 1-2 weeks due to excavation and backfill requirements.

Will waterproofing fix my basement mold problem?

Waterproofing removes the moisture source that mold needs to grow. Existing mold should be remediated, but preventing future water intrusion is essential to keeping mold from returning. A dry basement is a mold-resistant basement.

Do I need a permit for basement waterproofing?

Interior waterproofing typically doesn't require permits. Exterior waterproofing that involves excavation may require permits in some municipalities. We handle all necessary permitting as part of our service.

What areas do you serve for basement waterproofing?

We serve Detroit and all of Metro Detroit including Dearborn, Livonia, Warren, Southfield, Royal Oak, Troy, Farmington Hills, Ann Arbor, Sterling Heights, and surrounding communities in Wayne, Oakland, and Macomb counties.
