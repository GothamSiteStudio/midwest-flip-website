Our Kitchen Remodeling Process

A clear plan to keep your remodel moving with minimal disruption.

  1. Consult & Measure

    Review goals, measure the kitchen, and discuss layout, budget, and timing.

  2. Design & Selections

    Cabinets, counters, tile, lighting, appliances, and finishes are finalized.

  3. Permits & Prep

    Structural, electrical, and plumbing permits obtained; floors and paths protected.

  4. Demo & Rough

    Selective demo, framing, rough mechanicals, and inspections.

  5. Cabinets, Tops, Tile

    Cabinet set, counters templated/installed, tile backsplash set and grouted.

  6. Finish & Punch

    Appliance set, trim, paint, hardware, final clean, and walkthrough.

Kitchen Remodeling FAQ

Do you handle permits?

Yes. We prepare plans as needed and pull permits for structural, electrical, and plumbing work.

How long does a kitchen remodel take?

Typical timelines range from 4-10 weeks based on scope, lead times, and inspections.

Can you work with my designer?

Yes. We can build from your designer's plans or provide selection guidance in-house.

What about living in the home during the remodel?

We set up dust control, protect floors, and keep clear walk paths; some clients set up a temporary kitchen.

Do you install customer-supplied cabinets and appliances?

Yes, provided they are onsite before installation and compatible with the design.
