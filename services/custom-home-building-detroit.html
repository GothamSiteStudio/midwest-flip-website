Our Custom Home Building Process

  1. Discovery & Design

    We discuss your vision, needs, and budget. Our design team creates preliminary plans and 3D renderings for your review.

  2. Planning & Permits

    Finalize architectural drawings, select materials, and obtain all necessary permits from local municipalities.

  3. Site Preparation

    Clear the lot, grade the land, and prepare utilities. Foundation excavation begins based on soil conditions.

  4. Foundation & Framing

    Pour foundation, complete waterproofing, then frame walls, floors, and roof structure with precision.

  5. Mechanical Systems

    Install rough plumbing, electrical, HVAC ductwork, and any specialty systems before closing walls.

  6. Interior & Exterior Finishes

    Complete drywall, painting, flooring, cabinetry, countertops, siding, roofing, and all finish work.

  7. Final Details & Walkthrough

    Install fixtures, complete landscaping, pass final inspections, and conduct your comprehensive walkthrough.

Frequently Asked Questions About Custom Home Building in Detroit

How long does it take to build a custom home in Detroit?

Custom home construction typically takes 8-14 months depending on size, complexity, and design features. Larger luxury homes with unique architectural elements may take 12-18 months. We provide detailed timelines during the planning phase.

What is included in custom home building services?

Our custom home building includes complete design consultation, architectural planning, permit acquisition, site preparation, foundation work, framing, all mechanical systems (HVAC, electrical, plumbing), interior finishes, exterior work, landscaping, and final inspections.

Can I make changes during construction?

Yes, we accommodate changes during construction when possible. Early-stage modifications are easier to implement. Changes made after certain construction phases may affect timeline and budget, which we'll discuss transparently before proceeding.

Do you help with lot selection in Detroit?

Yes, we provide guidance on lot selection including evaluating soil conditions, zoning requirements, utility access, and neighborhood considerations. We can help assess potential building sites throughout the Detroit metro area.

What warranties do you provide?

We provide a comprehensive warranty on our workmanship, plus manufacturer warranties on all materials and systems. Specific warranty terms are detailed in your construction contract.

Can you build on difficult lots?

Yes, we have experience with challenging sites including sloped lots, waterfront properties, and lots with difficult soil conditions. We'll evaluate your site and design solutions that work with the terrain.

What areas do you serve for custom home building?

We build custom homes throughout Metro Detroit including Detroit, Dearborn, Livonia, Warren, Southfield, Royal Oak, Troy, Farmington Hills, Ann Arbor, Sterling Heights, and surrounding communities.
