Our Bathroom Remodeling Process

A clear, permit-ready sequence to keep your project moving and protected.

  1. Consultation & Scope

    Walk the existing bath, confirm goals, measure, and define budget/finish levels.

  2. Design & Selections

    Finalize layout, tile, fixtures, lighting, and ventilation. We clarify all line items.

  3. Permits & Prep

    Pull required permits and protect adjacent areas before demo begins.

  4. Demo & Rough

    Careful demolition, rough plumbing/electrical, blocking, and inspections.

  5. Waterproof & Tile

    Install backer, membranes, tile, grout, and sealant with clean lines and proper slope.

  6. Set Fixtures & Finish

    Vanity, tops, glass, trim, paint, and final punch so it is ready for daily use.

Bathroom Remodeling FAQ

Do I need permits for a bathroom remodel?

Yes, permits are typically required for plumbing, electrical, and structural changes. We handle the permit applications and inspections for you.

How long does a bathroom remodel take?

Most projects take 2-5 weeks depending on scope. Custom tile showers and layout changes add time for waterproofing and inspections.

Can you help with design and selections?

We guide layout, waterproofing details, and fixture selections. If you want a designer, we can coordinate with yours or recommend partners.

Do you install customer-supplied fixtures?

Yes, as long as items are compatible and on site before rough/finish stages. We can also source and manage materials for you.

What impacts cost the most?

Tile selections, layout changes, and plumbing/electrical upgrades drive cost. We provide options to fit your budget.
