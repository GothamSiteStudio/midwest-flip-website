Our Concrete Process

Predictable steps for a durable pour.

  1. Measure & Plan

    Confirm dimensions, thickness, reinforcement, and drainage.

  2. Excavate & Base

    Remove unsuitable soils; install and compact base stone.

  3. Form & Reinforce

    Set forms, reinforcement, and layout control joints.

  4. Pour & Finish

    Place concrete, finish to spec, and edge joints cleanly.

  5. Cure & Cleanup

    Apply cure methods, remove forms, and clean the site.

Concrete FAQ

How long before I can drive on it?

Light foot traffic is usually ok after 24 hours; vehicles after 5-7 days depending on weather.

Do you handle permits?

Yes, we manage concrete permits and inspections where required.

Do you offer stamped concrete?

Yes, with multiple patterns and release colors available.

How do you handle drainage?

We pitch slabs away from structures and toward approved drainage paths.

Can you replace only a section?

Yes, we can remove and replace failing panels as needed.
