Meet Midwest Flip LLC: Building Dreams in Detroit & Metro Detroit

Midwest Flip LLC Team January 6, 2026 5 min read

At Midwest Flip LLC, we believe every home tells a story. For over six years, we've been helping homeowners and investors throughout Detroit and Metro Detroit write new chapters in their homes‚Äîone renovation at a time.

Our Story

Midwest Flip LLC was founded with a simple mission: to bring quality craftsmanship, honest communication, and reliable service to residential construction in the Detroit area. What started as a passion for transforming spaces has grown into a trusted name in home remodeling, with over 400 completed projects across Wayne, Oakland, and Macomb counties.

We're not just contractors‚Äîwe're neighbors. Based in Detroit, we understand the unique character of our local homes, from the historic bungalows of Dearborn to the modern residences of Troy. We know the challenges that Michigan weather brings, the common issues in our region's housing stock, and the best solutions for lasting results.

Licensed & Insured for Your Peace of Mind

We hold a Michigan Residential Builder License, which covers building, replacing, repairing, altering, adding to, improving, and demolishing residential structures. This isn't just paperwork‚Äîit's our commitment to meeting state standards and protecting your investment.

Our Maintenance and Alteration credentials allow us to self-perform a wide range of work, including:

  • Carpentry and structural framing
  • Concrete work and masonry
  • Basement waterproofing
  • Excavation and site preparation
  • Insulation installation
  • Roofing, siding, and gutters
  • Window and door installation
  • Deck and porch construction
  • Interior finishing and trim work

For specialized trades like electrical, plumbing, and HVAC, we coordinate with licensed subcontractors we've vetted over the years. This ensures every aspect of your project meets code and is completed by qualified professionals.

Who We Serve

We proudly work with both homeowners and investors. Whether you're updating the kitchen in your forever home or flipping a property in the Metro Detroit market, we bring the same level of dedication and quality to every project.

Our Clients Include:

  • Homeowners looking to update, expand, or improve their living spaces
  • Real estate investors seeking reliable renovation partners for flip projects
  • Property managers needing maintenance and upgrades for rental properties
  • First-time buyers wanting to customize their new home

The Midwest Flip Difference

We stand behind our work. Every project comes with a 5-year workmanship warranty, giving you confidence that your investment is protected long after we've finished.

Clear Communication

No surprises. We provide written scopes of work, detailed line-item estimates, and clear timelines. You'll always know what's happening, when it's happening, and how much it costs.

Professional Process
