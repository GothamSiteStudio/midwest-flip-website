License, scope, and how we work

Residential Builder license

Michigan Residential Builder license covers building, replacing, repairing, altering, adding to, improving, or demolishing residential and mixed-use buildings (per LARA/State FAQ).

Maintenance & Alteration trades

We self-perform carpentry, concrete, excavation, siding/gutters, windows/doors, deck/porch work, and interior finishes. Electrical, plumbing, and HVAC are coordinated with licensed subcontractors.
